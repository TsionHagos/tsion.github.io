Lecture Content

Many people are born creative, but they lose their creative confidence over time. There are a number of reasons why people may lose their confidence: these reasons include the fear of the unknown, fear of being judged, fear of taking the first step, and fear of losing control. People can regain their creative confidence by taking small steps and building on their successes. They can also learn from their failures and use them as an opportunity to grow.
