About InsightBridge

InsightBridge is designed to help CIS students manage their pre-class workload more effectively. With access to readings, lecture notes, polls, and breakout questions, you can prioritize important concepts and boost your understanding. The self-explanation recorder also provides feedback to improve your accuracy and build confidence in class.
